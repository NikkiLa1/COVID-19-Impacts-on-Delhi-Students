<h1>COVID 19 Impacts on Delhi Students</h1>

<h2>Description</h2>
This project delves into the profound impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on students, particularly focusing on the Delhi National Capital Region (NCR). Using the dataset "COVID-19 Survey Student Responses.csv" from Kaggle, the study examines the mental health, education, and daily routines of 1182 students. By cleaning and organizing the data, including addressing outliers, the analysis unveils significant disruptions in students' lives, emphasizing altered sleeping patterns, fitness routines, and increased reliance on social media platforms like Instagram, Youtube, and WhatsApp. The project concludes by highlighting the importance of comprehensive support systems for students during global crises, drawing insights from the pandemic to inform future interventions.
